Bionic Biome is an independently developed arcade experience created and maintained by Pollito Productions.

The creation of Bionic Biome and the asociated app bundle was made possible through use of Gemini AI and the copiolot a GitHub codespace in this repository.

Effective February 19, 2026, in-app advertising has been implemented to support the continued development, maintenance, and expansion of Bionic Biome.

Implementation Details:

Ads are integrated in a user-conscious manner.

Rewarded advertisements are optional and provide in-game benefits (an extra life).

Advertisements will not interrupt active gameplay.
