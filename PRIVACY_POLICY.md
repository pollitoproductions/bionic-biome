# Privacy Policy for Bionic Biome

**Last Updated:** December 27, 2025

## 1. Introduction
Bionic Biome ("the App") is a game developed by Brant Fuller. This privacy policy describes how we handle your personal information.

## 2. Target Audience
**This App is intended for general audiences (users aged 13 and older).** We do not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

## 3. Data Collection and Ads
While the developer does not personally store your data, this App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

We use third-party advertising services (such as Google AdMob) to display ads. These services may collect and use data including:
* **Advertising ID:** A unique ID on your device used to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.
* **Device Information:** Battery level, device model, and operating system version.
* **Coarse Location:** Approximate location data to serve relevant ads.
* **Performance Data:** Crash logs and diagnostics to help us fix bugs.

## 4. Third-Party Service Providers
This App uses the following third-party services. By using the App, you agree to their privacy policies:
* [Google Play Services](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
* [AdMob](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543?hl=en)

## 5. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)
Depending on your location, you may have the right to request deletion of your data or opt-out of personalized advertising. You can typically manage your advertising preferences in your device settings under **Settings > Google > Ads**.

## 6. Contact
If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via the issue tracker on this GitHub repository.
